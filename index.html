Our Story

Mamtha Shopping Mall

Mamtha Shopping Mall, located at the heart of Kelambakkam on Market Road, has been serving the communities of Kittu Nagar, Ganapathy Nagar, and surrounding areas with a wide range of products including food items, home goods, and everyday essentials. With a warm and welcoming shopping environment, we pride ourselves on offering quality products at affordable prices, making every visit a convenient and enjoyable experience for families across the region.

Rated 4 out of 5 by over 1,288 satisfied customers, Mamtha Shopping Mall has earned the trust of thousands of households. Open every day from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM, we ensure that our shoppers always have access to the products they need, when they need them. Whether you are picking up daily groceries, household supplies, or looking for something special, our well-stocked shelves and friendly staff are here to assist you every step of the way.
